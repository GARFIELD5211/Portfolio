Cosmos Airline

A console-based airline simulation program built in C++, focused on customer service logic, booking systems, and user interaction. Implemented OOP principles like inheritance and encapsulation. This project helped me understand how real-world systems work behind the scenes.
“If you ever wanted to yell at airline support… now the airline responds.”

Kingdom Of Buria

A Mario-style 2D platformer game built with SFML and C++, featuring multiple levels, unique enemies, and 3 final bosses with special powers. Co-developed with friends, combining coding, storytelling, and game design. Focused on mechanics like camera following, map triggers, and boss behavior.
“Enter Buria. Survive the madness. And maybe punch a boss mid-monologue.”
